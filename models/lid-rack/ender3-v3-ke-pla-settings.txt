Lid Rack Print Settings

Printer: Creality Ender-3 V3 KE
Nozzle: 0.4 mm
Material: PLA (starting profile)
Part: lid-rack.scad / lid-rack.stl

Quality and Dimensions
- Layer height: 0.24 mm
- Line width: 0.42 mm
- Walls: 4 perimeters
- Top layers: 5
- Bottom layers: 5
- Infill: 20% (Gyroid or Grid)

Temperature
- Nozzle: 210 C (tune 205-215 C)
- Bed: 60 C

Speed
- Outer wall speed: 45 mm/s
- Inner wall speed: 80 mm/s
- General speed: up to 140 mm/s
- Initial layer speed: 20-25 mm/s

Cooling
- Fan: 100% after layer 3

Adhesion and Supports
- Build plate adhesion: Brim, 5 mm
- Supports: Off

Seam and Surface
- Z seam: Rear or aligned to one side

Part-Specific Adjustments
- Keep orientation flat on the bed (as modeled)
- Elephant foot compensation: 0.15-0.20 mm
- If slots are tight: increase slot gap by +0.4 to +0.8 mm
- If dividers are weak: increase divider_t from 3.0 to 3.6 mm

Notes
- This is a safe starting profile for this part on Ender-3 V3 KE.
- Fine-tune temperatures and speeds based on your PLA brand and room conditions.
